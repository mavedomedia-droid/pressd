FAQ

Quick answers.

How does pickup work?
Pickup is the first step. You request and pay for it through the app — ₦3,500 for Mainland or ₦4,500 for Island. That fee covers both the pickup and the delivery back to you. On the next available pickup day, a rider comes to collect your laundry. You can add Pressd Load or Pressd Care to your order, or just send what you have ready.
What days do you do pickups?
Pickups run on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays. When you request a pickup, you'll be scheduled for the next available day on that rotation.
How long until I get my clothes back?
Delivery happens 48–72 hours after pickup. Larger orders or detailed Care items can sit at the longer end of that window.
How does pricing work?
Pickup & delivery: ₦3,500 Mainland · ₦4,500 Island. One fee covers both directions.

Pressd Load: per-load pricing, see the pricing section above.

Pressd Care: per-item pricing with a ₦3,000 minimum tab. You'll see the full total before you pay.
Are the laundry services optional?
Yes. Pickup is the only required step. You can add Pressd Load and/or Pressd Care while requesting your pickup, or skip them and let the rider sort the rest in person.
How do I get help?
Talk to Donut, our 24/7 support agent on Telegram — @pressdlagosbot. Donut can take orders, answer questions, calculate costs, and pass anything tricky straight to support.
